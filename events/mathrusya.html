Mathrusya Old age & Child home is located near Tiruchanur, Andhra Pradesh. Here, kids of age range 6 to 15 years are provided with education. We know that children without parents are the most vulnerable people in the world. Also, the most terrible poverty is loneliness, and the feeling of being unloved. Mathrusya believed that little by little, bit by bit, family by family, so much good could be done on so many levels. This organization brings together all the kids who are eager to learn and help them achieve their heights. After all, the purpose of education is to replace the empty mind with an open one. So the children must be taught how to think, not what to think.
