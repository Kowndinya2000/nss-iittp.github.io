The government launched the Swachh Bharat mission in 2014 by Honourable Prime Minister Narendra Modi. The main aim of this mission is to make India free from open defecation. As part of the awareness program, we made them understand the harmful effects of plastic usage. However, to promote it, on January 29, 2020, we distributed jute bags to many households in Jangala Palle. We were constantly reminding them about the problems that come up with chopping of trees. The same day, the Swachh Bharat Campaign was also organized in the village, explaining the unpleasant effects of being careless about the surroundings. We have told the implications of open defecation to the villagers and tried to motivate them about the villageâ€™s cleanliness. The main objective was to create awareness about waste management and the importance of personal health and hygiene.

Information about the waste management systems, ways of segregating waste into dry and wet, the sort of materials that can be used instead of plastic, ways to reduce the usage of plastic were discussed with the villagers. We have also educated the villagers about the adverse impacts on health due to the disposal of waste in the surroundings, which would otherwise lead to groundwater contamination
