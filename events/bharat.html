As an initiative, IIT Tirupati did a household survey under UBA for five villages, namely Chindepalle, Panguru, and Rajulapalem, on February 16, 2020, Jangalapalle, Pagali on February 29, 2020, and found out various necessary details about the government schemes that they were availing. The survey report provides information about the problems of the village and helps in proper planning for village development. Some of them are the people’s poverty status, the waste management system that is being incorporated by them such as availability of proper sanitation facilities, availability of continuous power supply to the villages, type of cultivation and yield. The kind of cooking infrastructures like the electric, liquid gas, kerosene, or solar power employed by the villagers was also taken along with the village’s er facilities. All the above details enable us to know the people’s resent condition and thereby help us think of a technological solution for their development.

Our NSS team has conducted a survey and successfully delivered the importance of UBA in the villages. We have also distributed jute bags to the villagers. We sincerely thank the institution for provoking such an initiative and allowing us to serve the people.
