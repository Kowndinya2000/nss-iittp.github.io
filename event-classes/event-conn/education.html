ACTIVITIES UNDER EDUCATIONAL OUTREACH

The fundamental goal of these school activities’ primary goal is to ignite young minds and help them unravel the potential hidden within to create a better future for themselves and the people around them. We believe in breaking free from traditional age-old teaching methods and developing an innovative way of teaching children through interaction. In our sessions, we are not teachers, but friends, with whom they can laugh, learn, and savor the education. The below listed are the details of a few institutions or organizations that have been visited and the list of activities that have been done during the year 2019 - 20.

BRINGING ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE FROM ECONOMICALLY CHALLENGED

We aim to bring in the academic excellence from the economically challenged! We aim to encourage the spirit towards chasing their dreams and aspirations.

Navjeevan Blind School

EVEN WITHOUT SIGHT THERE IS STILL VISION

Blind schools are important institutions in imparting education among the visually impaired children in India. Special schools are the only place where blind children can be enabled to blend into an everyday life of dignity and self sufficiency. So we do our part to learn their struggles and help them a little to blend into the world.
