We are not just an organization!

NSS is an Opportunity for Youth to Contribute in Nation Building. NSS was introduced with the primary objective of developing the personality and character of the student youth through voluntary community service. NSS volunteers have been making very valuable contribution to the Society, besides developing their own personality.

  • NSS is a PAN India programme with more than 36.5 lakh NSS volunteers every year
  • NSS is spread across 39,465 Educational Institutions
  • Since its inception, over 4.78 crore students have benifited from NSS
